Featured Song Artist:

BEABADOBEE

Beaabadoobee is a rising star icon who is getting more and more fans because of her music. She is a Filipino-English singer & songwriter who is known for her unique blend of independent music. From bedroom-acoustic music, she can also do alternative and Indie music. Her career started when she wrote her first song on her guitar, which is titled "Coffee". The song became a hit which gained over 300,000 views on her Youtube. On April 2018, she signed a label by Dirty Hit Records. Her first tour was with Clairo on her Immunity Tour. Beabadobee released a lot of spotify singles and covers ever since then.

Her real name is Beatrice Kristi Ilejah Laus and she was born on June 3, 2000, in the Philippines. She moved to London when she was 3 years old. In the age of 17 she finally got her first second-hand guitar, after learning how to use the instrument for 7 years.

Beabadoobee's first debut EP "Lice" was made in March 2018. She also has a few of early EPs that are namely, "Patched Up", "Loveworm"Space Cadet" and her cover of "Don't you Forget About Me" originally by Simple Minds. When she released her third EP "Loveworm", she was placed on the NME's Annual List of "essential new artists".
